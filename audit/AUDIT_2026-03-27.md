**NIA Instruction Identification (Mixed):**

Three entries previously labeled as SECNAV Manuals have been re-identified as Naval Intelligence Activity (NIA) Instructions with the same SSIC numbers: NIAINST 1650.1 (NIA Awards Program), NIAINST 3820.1 (Intelligence Oversight Program), and NIAINST 5200.1 (NIA Audit Liaison Follow-up). These corrections are accurate — the source PDF contained these NIA instructions, not the similarly-numbered SECNAV Manuals. Dates are now correctly populated for all three.

The correction reveals, however, that the actual SECNAV policy manuals sharing these SSIC numbers were never in the source PDF:

- **SECNAV M-1650.1** (Navy and Marine Corps Awards Manual) — a major Fleet-wide reference governing the DoN awards program — is absent from the manifest.
- **SECNAV M-3820.1** (DoN Military Justice Manual) — governing military justice administration across the Navy and Marine Corps — is absent from the manifest.
- **SECNAV M-5200.1** (DoN Information Security Program Manual) — governing classified and controlled unclassified information handling — is absent from the manifest.

All three are active documents available through DONI and should be sourced and added to the manifest.

**Remaining Blank Dates:**

Two non-restricted entries still carry no publication date: CNAF M-3710.7 (NATOPS General Flight and Operating Instructions, 1 page — page count is also suspiciously low) and SECNAV M-5041.2 (DoN Research, Development, and Acquisition Procedures).
