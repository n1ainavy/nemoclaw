## Overview

This repository consolidates a comprehensive reference library of official U.S. Navy and Department of Defense regulatory documents. The library spans personnel management, military policy, joint operations, uniform regulations, and civilian workforce administration. Documents are drawn from authoritative Navy and DoD issuance systems and organized into nine distinct collections.

Each collection is drawn from a dedicated sub-manifest file and covers a specific issuing authority or regulatory domain.

## Collection Descriptions

### BUPERS Instructions
**98 documents | ~2,134 source pages**

Instructions issued by the Bureau of Naval Personnel (BUPERS/NAVPERS). Topics include:
- Enlisted and officer personnel administration
- Navy corrections and confinement facilities
- Performance evaluation (NAVFIT)
- Civilian workforce HR programs (leave, overtime, grievances, awards)
- Records management and the electronic military personnel records system (eRMS)
- Special programs (Astronaut Candidate, White House Fellows, Legislative Fellows, Nuclear Propulsion)
- Information management and cybersecurity

---

### CJCS
**163 documents | ~7,092 source pages**

Instructions and manuals issued by the Chairman of the Joint Chiefs of Staff. Topics include:
- Joint force planning and execution (JOPES, global force management)
- Joint professional military education (JPME/PME) policy
- Multinational and NATO operations
- Operational reporting, lessons learned, and combat assessment
- Information operations, cyber, and mission assurance
- International agreements and arms control compliance

---

### DoD / DoW
**92 documents**

Directives, instructions, and publications from the Department of Defense and Department of the Navy, establishing overarching policy applicable across all military services.

---

### MILPERSMAN
**821 documents**

The Military Personnel Manual is the largest single collection and serves as the primary day-to-day policy reference for Navy personnel administration. Articles are organized numerically and cover the full lifecycle of enlisted and officer service, including accession, assignment, advancement, performance, separation, and benefits.

---

### OPNAV Instructions
**366 documents**

Instructions issued by the Office of the Chief of Naval Operations (OPNAV), governing Navy-wide programs, warfare readiness, training standards, and administrative policy across the fleet.

---

### RESPERSMAN
**43 documents**

The Reserve Personnel Manual contains policies and procedures specific to Navy Reserve personnel, including drilling reservists, Individual Ready Reserve (IRR), and mobilization processes.

---

### SECNAV Instructions
**218 documents**

Instructions issued by the Secretary of the Navy establishing department-level policy for both the Navy and Marine Corps on matters including personnel, acquisition, legal, financial management, and public affairs.

---

### SECNAV Manuals
**35 documents**

Manuals issued by the Secretary of the Navy providing detailed procedural guidance that supplements SECNAV Instructions. These are typically more comprehensive reference documents governing specific functional areas.

---

### Uniform Regulations
**109 articles**

The U.S. Navy Uniform Regulations, organized by chapter and article, sourced from [mynavyhr.navy.mil](https://www.mynavyhr.navy.mil/References/US-Navy-Uniforms/Uniform-Regulations/). Chapters include:

| Chapter | Scope |
|---|---|
| 1 | General provisions and authority |
| 2 | Officer uniforms |
| 3 | Warrant Officer uniforms |
| 4 | Junior Enlisted (E1–E6) uniforms |
| 5 | Insignia and devices |
| 6 | Chief Petty Officer (E7+) uniforms |
| 7 | Staff Corps and special programs |
| 8 | Special uniform situations (maternity, joint duty, retired, reserve) |
| 9 | Additional uniform items (headgear, footwear, outerwear, cold weather gear) |
